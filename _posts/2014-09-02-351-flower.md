## How the game works
<p>
<ul>
<li>A dragonfly on screen flies around to 6 different flowers. The player must click the flower that the dragonfly went to n-flowers ago, where n is specified by the level.</li>
<li>The dragonfly lands on a flower for 2 seconds. The user has 2 seconds to respond to what the previous flower was.</li>
<li>The game lasts for 24 seconds on level 1. 4 seconds are added at each level after level 1.</li>
<li>12 stimuli are displayed on the first level. 2 more are added at every level after level 1.</li>
<li>The answer to the first stimuli is a non-response as the dragonfly was not on any stimuli 1-back ago.</li>
</ul>
</p>
